About Us

Hello there. One of us is Jess, and the other one of us is Mark. We're nerds who play traditional (kind of) music together. Jess is best known right now for playing in contra dance band Pepperjack, and Mark is best known for writing a bunch of tunes lately. Our music has grown together over the last decade and so here's a site for all that.
